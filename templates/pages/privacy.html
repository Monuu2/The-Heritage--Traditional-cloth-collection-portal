PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account (name, email, phone number, profile photo), placing orders, and contacting us. We also collect usage data such as pages visited and search queries to improve our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for processing orders, improving our services, sending order confirmations and updates, and providing customer support. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share limited information with trusted logistics partners solely for the purpose of delivering your orders. All partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption and hashed passwords to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at heritage@culture.com. We will respond within 14 business days.

6. Cookies

We use session cookies to maintain your login state and cart. These are essential for the platform to function correctly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at theheritagesite319@gmail.com.
